WIYF: What's in Your Fridge?

An app that offers recipe options based on ingredients already in your fridge with the hopes of reducing food waste.

  • Brainstorming
  • User Research
  • Ideation Design
  • Usability Testing
  • Prototyping
  • Conclusion

In this research project, a series of interview questions around meal prep at home, grocery shopping habits, and cooking pain points was created.


User research was conducted and found that people cook at home several times a week and like to follow a recipe, however, those same users find that a lack of time and energy prevents them from making meals.

Motivate home chefs by suggesting recipes to them that use the ingredients they already have in their kitchen.
